# HHE Directory

### A practitioner directory built for HHE students, not the internet.

**Live now**: [hhe-directory.vercel.app](https://hhe-directory.vercel.app)

---

## What you saw today

**1. A real front door.** A search-first landing page that says "Find a practitioner you can trust" and means it. 18 HHE-trained practitioners across 13 cities — Atlanta, Savannah, Athens, Macon, Asheville, Boulder, Sedona, and more. Every one of them looks like the kind of person an HHE graduate or referring patient would actually want to find.

**2. Search that helps, not sorts.** Type a specialty — "hormone," "gut health," "functional medicine." Filter by city. Combine filters. The system shows you only what's possible from where you are; it doesn't dump a directory tree and ask you to guess. Typos still work. The mobile experience is built for the way real patients actually search.

**3. A profile that earns trust.** Click into any practitioner and you get a Linktree-style page — name, city, specialty, bio. The action buttons ("Book an intro consult," "Browse offerings," "Request custom invoice") are placeholders for now; they ship in Phase 2 alongside payments + booking infrastructure.

---

## Why this isn't a phonebook

This is the core wedge: every practitioner here is HHE-curated. Not scraped from a national license database. Not stuffed with paid SEO ranking. **Invite-only.** Your students see practitioners trained in the framework you teach — functional medicine, gut health, hormone balance, mind-body work, herbal medicine. The directory's tagline isn't a marketing claim; it's a structural decision baked into how listings get added.
